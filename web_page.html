THE AURA SENTRY BAND - A SMART WEARABLE GUARDIAN

A HEALTH MONITORING AND PREDECTIVE ALERT SYSTEM

"Watchful Eye For Those You Love - A Wristband That NEVER SLEEPS!!"

This is a smart wristband designed to be a personal guardian.
It constantly monitors the wearer's vital signs and surroundings through various sensors. All the user's activity data is sent to a mobile app, which analyzes it and provides real-time alerts to
both the user and their caretaker in case of any danger, ensuring quick action.

Special Functions

  1. Multi-Sensor Monitoring: Tracks physiological factors like Heart Rate, Blood Oxygen (SpO2), Body Temperature, and body movements.
  2. Emergency Detection: Intelligently detects falls, strokes (through irregular movements), drowning (via submersion), and hazardous gases in the air.
  3. Emotion & Stress Recognition: Analyzes data patterns to infer emotional states like high stress or anxiety.
  4. GPS with Auto-Alert: The built-in GPS tracks location. In a critical emergency, the band automatically makes an SOS call to the caretaker and shares the live location instantly.

Most Unique Keyfeatures

  1. Caretaker Band: Paired band for caretakers, allowing for two-way monitoring and alerts between the user and their guardian.
  2. Group Connectivity: Enabling multiple bands to connect, ideal for monitoring family members or groups in outdoor activities.
  3. Self-Charging (Energy Harvesting): A system that automatically charges the device using body heat and solar energy, making it more sustainable.
